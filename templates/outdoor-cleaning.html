Outdoor Cleaning

The exterior of your property is the first impression guests or clients have of your home or business. At Clean Works Hospitality, we offer a comprehensive range of outdoor cleaning services to ensure your property's exterior is as clean and inviting as the interior. From pressure washing to patio cleaning and window washing, we have the tools and expertise to transform your outdoor spaces. Our outdoor cleaning services not only enhance the curb appeal of your property but also help maintain its integrity and longevity. Our team is experienced in tackling outdoor cleaning projects of all sizes and complexities. We use environmentally friendly cleaning agents and the latest equipment to ensure efficient and effective cleaning without harming the environment.

  • Pressure washing of exterior surfaces (siding, deck, patio)
  • Cleaning and scrubbing of outdoor furniture
  • Window washing (exterior)
  • Removal of dirt, mold, and mildew from outdoor surfaces
  • Driveway and sidewalk cleaning
  • Cleaning and maintenance of outdoor fixtures (lighting, grills, etc.)
  • Pool area cleaning and maintenance (if applicable)
  • Fencing cleaning and maintenance
