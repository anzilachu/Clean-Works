Deep Cleaning Services

Sometimes, a standard cleaning just isn't enough to get your space truly clean and healthy. That's where our deep cleaning services come in. At Clean Works Hospitality, we specialize in going the extra mile to eliminate hidden dirt, allergens, and contaminants that can accumulate over time. Our deep cleaning process includes a comprehensive cleaning of high-traffic areas, kitchen appliances, bathroom fixtures, and more. We pay attention to every detail to ensure your space is not only clean but also sanitized and refreshed. Our deep cleaning services are ideal for those who want to give their home or business a thorough and rejuvenating makeover. We use advanced cleaning techniques and eco-friendly products to ensure a deep clean that's safe for your health and the environment.

  • Detailed cleaning of kitchen appliances (inside and out)
  • Grout and tile cleaning
  • Carpet cleaning or deep vacuuming
  • Upholstery cleaning
  • Cleaning of light fixtures and ceiling fans
  • Cleaning and sanitization of switches and doorknobs
  • Cleaning and disinfection of vents and air ducts
  • Inside and outside window cleaning
  • Cleaning and polishing of wood and other hard surfaces
  • Deep cleaning of blinds and curtains
